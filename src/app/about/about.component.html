About

This project consists of the design and development of an advanced HR application designed to help streamline time tracking and management for teams and organizations.

The application integrates with Jira, which is a project management tool commonly used by developers, to allow them to automatically track their time spent on specific tasks.

The application also includes a built-in time logging feature to allow users to log their time directly in the application.

The application includes a user management system that allows HR and project managers to easily add, remove, and edit users and their access rights.

Additionally, the day off request feature allows employees to easily request time off and view their approved schedule.

The application also includes reporting features to provide insights into employee time and attendance data, as well as security measures to ensure the protection of sensitive employee data.

The application is mobile compatible, scalable, and integrates with other business systems, making it a comprehensive solution for managing and optimizing team efficiency.
